1. What are the methods for calculating GDP? Please list the formulas used in these methods. / 核算GDP的方法有哪些，请列出这些方法用到的公式。

Expenditure method（支出法）: GDP = C + I + G + NX

Income method （收入法）: GDP = Wage + Interest + Rent + Profit + Depreciation + Indirect tax - Subsidy

2. What are the defects of GDP indicators? / GDP指标的缺陷

  • (1) Unable to reflect the status of income distribution; / 不能反映收入分配收入分配状况；
  • (2) Neglecting housework and underground economic factors; / 忽略了家务劳动和地下经济因素；
  • (3) It can not reflect the cost paid by the economy for the mode of economic growth; / 不能反映经济体为经济增长方式付出的代价；
  • (4) It can not reflect people’s quality of life. / 不能反映人们的生活质量。

3. What are the factors that affect the expected return? / 影响预期收益的因素有哪些？

  • Expectations of the demand for investment project products; / 对投资项目产品的需求预期；
  • Product cost; / 产品成本；
  • Investment tax credit; / 投资税抵免；
  • Investment risk; / 投资风险；
  • The impact of financing conditions on investment demand. / 融资条件对投资需求的影响。

4. What are the government related multipliers? / 与政府相关的乘数？

  • (1) Multiplier of government purchase expenditure; kg = 1 / (1 - β)
  • (2) Tax multiplier; kt = -β / (1 - β) (Note: User provided kt=1/1-β, which is usually for government spending. Tax multiplier is typically negative and includes MPC)
  • (3) Government transfer payment multiplier; ktr = β / (1 - β)
  • (4) Balance budget multiplier. Kb = 1

5. What are the motives for money demand? / 货币需求的动机是什么？

Money demand is the need for people to hold a certain amount of money under different conditions for various considerations. / 货币需求就是人们在不同的条件下出于各种考虑而愿意持有一定数量的货币的需要。

  • (1) Transaction motivation; / 交易动机；
  • (2) Preventive motivation; / 预防动机；
  • (3) Speculative motives. / 投机动机。

6. What are the reasons for wage stickiness? (P140) / 工资粘性的原因是什么？

  • On labor wage contract; / 劳动工资合同论；
  • Implicit contract theory; / 隐含合同论；
  • Insider outsider theory; / 局内人-局外人理论；
  • Efficiency wage theory. / 效率工资理论。

7. Causes of inflation. (P145) / 通货膨胀的原因。

  • (1) Demand pull-up type; / 需求拉上型；
  • (2) Cost driven; / 成本推动型；
  • (3) Structural type; / 结构型；
  • (4) Excessive money supply / 货币供给过多

8. Main characteristics of business cycle. (P166) / 经济周期的主要特征

  • (1) Business cycle is an inevitable fluctuation in the operation of market economy; / （1）经济周期是市场经济运行中不可避免的波动；
  • (2) It is the fluctuation of the overall economy; / （2）是总体经济的波动；
  • (3) It can be divided into four stages: prosperity, recession, depression and recovery; / （3）可以分为繁荣、衰退、萧条、复苏四个阶段；
  • (4) There are great differences in the length of an economic cycle. / （4）一个经济周期时间长短存在较大的差别。

9. What are the objectives of macroeconomic policy? (P205) / 宏观经济政策的目标是什么？

  • (1) Full employment / 充分就业
  • (2) price stability / 物价稳定
  • (3) economic growth / 经济增长
  • (4) balance of payments / 国际收支平衡

10. What are the automatic stabilizers and what are the main ones? (P213) / 什么是自动稳定器，主要有哪些？

The automatic stabilizer is a mechanism for the economic system itself to reduce the impact and interference on national income. / 自动稳定器是经济系统本身存在一种减少对国民收入冲击和干扰的机制。

  • Unemployment insurance mechanism; / 包括失业保障机制；
  • Agricultural product price maintenance system; / 农产品价格维持制度；
  • Income tax system. / 所得税税收体系。

11. According to the theory of economic growth, how to promote economic growth to achieve sustainable development? / 根据经济增长理论，如何促进经济增长实现可持续发展？

Y = AF(N, K)

  • Encourage technological progress; / 鼓励技术进步；
  • Encourage capital formation; / 鼓励资本形成；
  • Increase labor supply; / 增加劳动供给；
  • Establish appropriate systems. / 建立适宜的制度。

12. Please elaborate the combination of fiscal policy and monetary policy in 2025, and explain its impact on national income and price level in combination with IS-LM model and AD-AS model? / 请阐述2025年财政政策和货币政策的组合，并结合IS-LM模型和AD-AS模型说明其对国民收入和价格水平的影响？

Active fiscal policy and moderately loose monetary policy. The IS curve and LM Curve move to the right, thus moving the AD curve to the right, further increasing the price level and income. / 积极的财政政策和适度宽松的货币政策。IS曲线和LM曲线右移，从而使AD曲线右移，进一步提高价格水平和收入。
